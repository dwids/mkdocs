    What is Lot Liability?

    Lot Liability

    "represents the share of owners corporation expenses that each lot owner is required to pay....These ... liabilities are determined by the developer at the time of subdivision." Source Consumer Vic website

    It's basically a form of percentage. You can see it adds up to 1000 'units' (AH; right term?). I (David) just divide them by 10 to get a percentage

    So if my Lot Liability is 42, that's 

    42/10 = 4.2%

or 0.042
